About me

Do you know how it feels to be truly and completely passionate about a topic? I didn't until I fell in love with coding and its applications within computer graphics and simulation. I've always had a great interest in mathematics, logic and problem solving and this made me start studing Engineering Physics at KTH in Stockholm.

At KTH I went to my first class in computer programming and everything fell into place. Coding managed to combine both my technical and creative side and I started doing lots of coding in my free time. I went on to a semester abroad in Dublin where I did my degree project by programming a small self driven vehicle and realised that I wanted to dive full on into the coding world and especially its applications within computer graphics and simulations.

Therefor I started studying MSc in Media Technology and Engineering at Linköping Universityand now I get to spend everyday learning about topics that drives me to learn and explore more. I want to inspire more people, and especially girls, to try out coding and I get to do that both by working as a Student Ambassador and as a blogger at the non-profit organisation Datatjej. I dream about a future where I get to work with coding towards simulations and rendering within VFX, games or scientific visualization.
